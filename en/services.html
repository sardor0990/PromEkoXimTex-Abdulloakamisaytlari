Services

Our company provides the following services:

  • Conducting sleeve, dense loading of the main and protective layers of catalysts under the supervision of a representative of the manufacturer of the catalyst (if required), in accordance with the reactor loading scheme, at the customer's site.
  • Regeneration of catalysts at specialized sites of the Russian Federation and the European Union in order to restore the activity of catalytic properties by removing hydrocarbons from the catalyst surface.
